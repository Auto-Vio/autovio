# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in AutoVio a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions, issues, and pull requests
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users
- Help newcomers get oriented

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Dismissing or belittling contributions
- Spam or off-topic promotion

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers via GitHub. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately. Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments or ban participants who violate these standards.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other official AutoVio channels.
